END TO END WORKERS helps us to save our valuable time and money and let us do things we love, by fixing all our maintenance worries with just a click of a button. This system will be a service like never before. This project contains 4 users. Admin will control the entire system and he will evaluate and verify the service providers who have certain employees under his control and he provide the employees for customers as per their needs. The main objective of the system is to connect customers to service providers for different services like repair and maintenance. It brings together a host of services under one roof. The aim of this system is to leveraging the on-demand economy and eliminating the pain in finding a professional. The company evaluates the eligibility of all enlisted service providers so that you can rest your worries. They also provide additional training and counselling for bridging any gap that exists in their service. This system has the following objectives: to maintain simple database, to maintain easy user interface that is user friendly and attractive and to maintain the speed of system functionality.
